Rapid growth of Partnership Development

PN Cambodia is also lucky to benefit from the skills and abilities of its partners. Smart Axiata recently chose to support PNC and offered to provide students with some additional digital knowledge for their future career paths and secure IT human resources for tomorrow’s human capital.


Furthermore, internships are also a part of our training programme to improve our students’ hard and soft skills, through practice and immersion in the professional world. According to the report on our PNC class 2019’s internships, 24 partner companies welcomed our 49 students for a four-month full-time internship. Over 37% offered our students the chance of a full-time job after their internship. It was a great support to give opportunities to Cambodian underprivileged young people.
